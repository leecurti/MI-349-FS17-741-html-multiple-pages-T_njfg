Squirtle (Japanese: ゼニガメ Zenigame)

Squirtle is a Water-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I.It evolves into Wartortle starting at level 16, which evolves into Blastoise starting at level 36. Along with Bulbasaur and Charmander, Squirtle is one of three starter Pokémon of Kanto available at the beginning of Pokémon Red, Green, Blue, FireRed, and LeafGreen.
